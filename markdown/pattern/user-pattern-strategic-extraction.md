# Strategic Extraction - Institutional Containment as Resource Cover

Sophisticated navigation pattern for politically constrained technical environment: Use institutional containment as cover for building desired work on employer resources while maintaining external credibility and optionality. Not defensive posture but conscious resource optimization when internal mobility blocked and technical excellence creates political vulnerability.

## Pattern Components

**1. Minimal viable compliance**
- Meet "satisfactory expectations" bar at minimum cost
- UO bar: One ticket/week + moderately responsive on Teams
- Deliberately low threshold enables maximum energy redirect

**2. Resource arbitrage**
- Topped-out AP3 salary as stable income
- Work time/infrastructure for PDS/MCP development
- Institutional affiliation for conference credibility
- "On their dime" while building external portfolio

**3. External credibility building**
- Conference presentations (even if out-of-pocket)
- Open advising to other institutions
- Code sharing and implementation guidance
- "Contribution to higher ed" framing (genuine + strategic)

**4. Optionality maintenance**
- External validation creates exit options
- Internal stalemate sustainable if softens toward equilibrium
- No forced timeline, can ride out or pivot based on signals

**5. Constant vigilance discipline**
- "Mental framing and discipline in action/communication"
- Every Teams message, meeting, interaction filtered through exposure risk
- High-tax cost but necessary for stalemate sustainability

## When to Apply

**Indicators this pattern fits:**
- Technical excellence creates political vulnerability (exposes incompetence, contradicts leadership roadmap)
- Internal mobility blocked (blackballing evidence, manufactured HR complaints)
- External validation available (other orgs adopting your work)
- Financial stability adequate (topped-out classification, stable salary)
- Internal constraint tolerable with energy redirect (satisfactory bar achievable at low cost)

**When pattern doesn't fit:**
- Can't meet minimum bar without burning out
- Financial instability requires advancement
- External validation not materializing
- Psychological cost of vigilance unsustainable
- Institutional dysfunction actively deteriorating rather than softening

## Sustainability Calculus

**Benefits:**
- Salary + platform for development + advising reach
- Time to build external credibility before forced exit
- Avoid retaliatory reference/exit complications
- Institutional affiliation credibility while building portfolio

**Costs:**
- Constant vigilance on communication/action (high tax)
- Limited internal advancement opportunity
- Dependency on "softening" continuing vs. erosion
- Team relationship damage from past conflicts

**Monitoring signals:**
- Softening: Reduced micromanagement, team lead offer (even if declined), working relationship "better"
- Erosion: New HR incidents, resource denial escalation, reimbursement ghosting, additional hiring blocks

**Exit threshold:** When costs exceed benefits or external opportunity compelling enough to leave stable extraction

## Execution Discipline

**Communication:**
- Filter all messages through "will this create exposure?"
- No technical advocacy that contradicts leadership roadmap
- Document everything (defensive record-keeping)
- Minimize after-hours communication (even though everyone does it)

**Work allocation:**
- One ticket/week minimum for visibility
- Teams responsiveness moderate (not invisible, not over-eager)
- PDS/MCP development during work time but framed as personal
- External advising on own time/dime (conference out-of-pocket)

**Political navigation:**
- Don't correct leadership incompetence publicly
- Let external validation speak (other schools adopting PDS)
- Decline poisoned chalices (team lead for clericals)
- Maintain "better" working relationship without trust restoration

## Brock's Application (2025-11-22)

**Current state:** "High-tax but softening from all sides, waiting to see if stabilizes into long-term equilibrium"

**Applied for:** ~3 months since Sept 2024 HR letter

**Signals:**
- Working relationship with Travis "better"
- Team lead offer from Derek (declined but indicates not fully blackballed)
- No new HR incidents
- Conference presentation successful (external schools responding)

**Risks:**
- Reimbursement ghosting ($2K unpaid)
- Internal hiring block (customer dept rejected him)
- Team rift unresolved (trust damaged)

**Horizon:** Indefinite - "We'll see" if stabilizes or erodes

## Related Patterns

**Strategic patience vs. active advocacy:** This is strategic patience extreme - advocacy fully external while internal profile minimized

**Dual infrastructure navigation:** Related but broader - this pattern specifically optimizes resource extraction from constraining institution

**Institutional antibodies:** This pattern assumes antibodies already activated, focuses on sustainable stalemate rather than adoption campaign

## Anti-Patterns

**Passive resignation:** This isn't giving up, it's active resource optimization

**Martyrdom:** Not proving point or fighting for principle, pragmatic extraction

**Stealth escalation:** Not secretly building case for revenge/exposure, building external portfolio for optionality

**Checked-out:** Still delivering satisfactory work, just not exceeding or advocating internally

## Success Metrics

**Internal:**
- No new HR incidents
- Performance ratings remain satisfactory
- Salary/benefits maintained
- Constraints don't tighten further

**External:**
- Conference invitations increasing
- School adoptions growing
- Implementation guidance requests rising
- Consulting/exit options materializing

**Sustainability:**
- Vigilance tax tolerable (not burning out)
- "Softening" continues (working relationship stable/improving)
- No forced timeline pressure (financial runway adequate)
- Psychological equilibrium maintained (frustration manageable)

**Exit readiness:**
- External credibility sufficient for transition
- Portfolio demonstrates capability
- References available outside current management
- Financial runway for job search if needed
